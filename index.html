Live tribute walls for every celebration

A living wall of love, on the big screen.

Guests scan a code or tap a tablet, leave a message or photo, and it appears live on screen — a wall of tributes building all night for the person who matters. Weddings, big birthdays, retirements, memorials and more.

No app for guests · Works on any phone · Your event, your colours.
How it works

Three steps to a wall of love

Set it up in minutes. Guests do the rest.

1

Create the event

Name the guest of honour, pick the occasion, choose your colours. Your wall is ready instantly.

2

Guests leave tributes

Share a QR code or stand a tablet at the door. Guests add a message or photo in seconds — no app needed.

3

Watch it come alive

Open the wall on the venue screen. Every tribute floats in live, all evening long.
