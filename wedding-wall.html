How it works — scan, post, big screen

Three effortless steps, from your invitations to your wedding night to a keepsake you'll hold forever.

1

You share a QR code

Print it on table cards, slip it into invitations, or display it on a sign at the venue entrance. Your guests see it the moment they arrive.

2

Guests scan and post

One tap opens the page — no app to install, no account to create. They post photos, heartfelt messages, and all the love they brought with them, instantly, from any phone.

3

It all appears live

Every post flows onto the big screen at your venue in real time. And every single photo and message is saved in your wall — yours to download and keep forever.

Questions about your wedding wall

Do guests need to download an app?
No app needed. Guests scan a QR code with any smartphone camera and post instantly — nothing to install.
Does it work on any phone?
Yes — GatherWall works on any modern smartphone, iOS or Android, with no account or app required.
Can we keep all the photos afterwards?
Absolutely. Every photo and message your guests post is saved, and you get a download of everything after the event.
Can we brand the wall with our names and colours?
Yes — you can personalise the wall with your names, wedding date, and a colour scheme that matches your day.
What is The Song of Your Night?
At the end of your event, GatherWall reads every message your guests left and turns their words into an original song — a unique keepsake made from your evening together.
