A few Czech sounds, quickly

č=ch (church) · š=sh (shoe) · ž=zh (measure) · c=ts (cats) · j=y (yes) · ch=throaty h (loch).

ř is the famous one — a rolled r + zh together. Don't worry if it's rough at first.

Stress is almost always on the first syllable. Accents (á, í, ý…) just hold the vowel longer.
