The story behind the song
I

Where it began

Every keepsake starts with a conversation. We ask about the early years — the town she grew up in, the dreams she carried, the person she was before she was "Mum."

II

The years she gave

The late nights, the sacrifices, the quiet strength. We weave the real moments into the lyrics, so the song feels less like a tribute and more like a memory set to music.

III

The family she built

Names, milestones, the in-jokes only this family shares. Each detail you give us becomes a line — a song no one else in the world could have.

IV

The celebration

On the day, it plays on the big screen as her story unfolds — and afterwards, it's hers to keep forever. A gift that doesn't end when the party does.
