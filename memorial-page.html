Questions

Frequently asked questions

Is the memorial page private?
You control who can see it — share just a link with family, or make it open to all who knew them.
How do family members add their memories?
Anyone with the link can add a memory, a photo, or a message — no account needed.
How long does the page stay online?
Your memorial stays online for as long as you need. There are no annual renewal fees in the base plan.
Can people light a candle?
Yes — every memorial includes a candle-lighting feature, so visitors can mark their remembrance.
Do you store our personal data?
GatherWall requires no account to build or view a memorial. We store only what you give us to create the page.
