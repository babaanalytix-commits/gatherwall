A Wall of Love
GatherWall · Ode for the one, Live for the many
Turn the sound up 🎧

It began with one wall, for one person. This is the song that grew from it — for every name we whisper and every room that sings as one. — from all of us at GatherWall

The words

Verse 1

For the one you hold the dearest,
for a love that words can't say —
we'll set a whole life to a melody
that never fades away.

Pre-chorus

Every photo, every line,
every voice across the miles,
come and lay it down tonight…

Chorus

This is a wall of love,
and the moment carries on —
gather round, gather round,
till the many feel like one.
From a single beating heart
to a room that sings along,
a living wall of love,
where we all belong.

Verse 2

No app — just scan, and you're with us,
add your heart to the screen;
watch a hundred little moments
become the night we've seen.

Bridge

Light a candle, raise a glass,
let the quiet say their name —
what money never could buy:
love, remembered, stays the same.
(Ode for the one… Live for the many…)

Final chorus

This is a wall of love,
and the moment carries on —
gather round, gather round,
for the ones worth celebrating.
From a single beating heart
to a room that sings along,
a living wall of love,
for everyone.
